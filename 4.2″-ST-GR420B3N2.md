# Pins to the EPaper Display (SSD1619) #
ZBS243 Pin                       |EPD       | Note             
:-------------------------:|:-------------------------:|:-------------------------:
P1.7 | _CS | Active low
P2.1 | Busy | Active High
P2.2 | D/C | High for data, low for commands
P2.0 | _RESET | Active low
P0.0 | SPI_CLK
P0.1 | MOSI/MISO | Used for both; same pin to read data
